About Me

 For nearly a decade, Denis Molloy has been directly responsible for the release of over 650 retail home entertaiment products such as video games, DVD and Blu ray home videos, and language-learning software.
 After moving from Seattle, WA to Cary, NC, Denis wanted to push himself and his career to the next level. To achieve this goal, he enrolled in The Coding Boot Camp at UNC-Chapel Hill...an intensive, six-month full stack coding bootcamp in order to become a Web Developer.
 In his spare time, Denis loves spending as much time as possible in the outdoors. Whether hiking trails, setting up camp, or simply taking pictures, he loves to live life to the fullest and create as many experiences as possible.
